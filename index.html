Mission Statement

Our Mission at Roots-Staffing Care and Support Agency is to provide compassionate and personalized care services that empower individuals to live with dignity, independence, and fulfillment.

R - Respect

We respect every individual's unique needs and preferences, and provide personalized care services that promote independence, dignity, and well-being.

O - Open Communication

We believe in open communication and build strong relationships with our clients and their families based on trust, respect, and empathy.

O - Outstanding Service

We are committed to delivering high-quality care services that exceed expectations and set the standard for excellence in the industry.

T - Tailored Care Plans

We create tailored care plans that meet the specific needs of each individual and their family, and adjust them as needed to ensure the best possible outcomes.

S - Supportive Environment

We strive to create a supportive and nurturing environment that promotes physical, emotional, and social well-being for our clients and their families.

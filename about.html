Who are we?

Our Story

Roots-Staffing is a privately owned recruitment company that specializes in providing well-trained and skilled personnel in various care settings. We have a team of compassionate professionals who prioritize the needs and preferences of our clients. Our ultimate goal is to place our employees in suitable working environments where they can contribute to the care of those in need.


Our company offers a range of services that includes staffing for nursing homes, residential homes, supported living care, private hospitals, learning disabilities, and physical disabilities. We understand the unique needs and requirements of each care setting and we ensure that our staff are trained and equipped to meet these needs.


At Roots-Staffing, we pride ourselves on our exceptional service delivery and aim to exceed our clients' expectations. We offer flexible shifts to ensure that our clients receive care whenever they need it. We also guarantee prompt weekly payments to our staff to show our appreciation for their hard work and dedication. Our company provides 24/7 out of hours support to ensure that our clients and staff have access to immediate assistance whenever they require it. We value our employees' personal and professional growth and offer opportunities for them to progress in their careers.
